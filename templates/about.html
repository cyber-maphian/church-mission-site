About
Gospel explosion mission

We're God's children and ambassadors in different spheres of influence. We are burdened with the heartbeat of God, especially to see the lost restored unto God. We have strange passion to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ to the unreached people of all nations. We prioritize the assignment of preaching the good news, which is the message of God's love above all forms of personal and collective responsibilities as we passionately burn with the burden of Christ.

We hold a special connection to God, like children entrusted with important roles in different areas of life. We deeply feel God's desire for all people to find their way back to Him, especially those who haven't yet encountered His love.

Evangelist
Olamilekan Busayo

A devoted evangelist who is dedicated and committed to spreading the message of truth and salvation across nations and diverse cultures. He is a contemporary evangelist and also a dynamic spiritual leader whose life's mission revolves around sharing the gospel and fostering spiritual growth.

Evangelist Busayo is also armed with theological education (Dunamis School Of Ministry) and a fervent spirit. His impact is evident in the transformed lives of individuals who credit his ministry for guiding them in their spiritual journeys. He is committed to community outreaches, humanitarian efforts and fostering inclusivity within the church has solidified a legacy that extends beyond the pulpit, he stands a living testament to the enduring relevance of evangelism in the contemporary world.

In conclusion Evangelist Olamilekan Busayo is happily married to Damilola Ruth and they are both blessed with a daughter.He is a beacon of inspiration for those seeking spiritual guidance as he continues to shape lives through his ministry.
